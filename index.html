Episode One

DENVER — The city’s mile-high altitude really hits the brothers hard in our first installment of Data Rich, a podcast about data journalism. This month the brothers attend NICAR, the annual conference for the National Institute of Computer-Assisted Reporting. It's the oldest and largest conference dedicated to data journalism and a great place to talk to old friends and new about data journalism and its evolution. Enjoy!

Friends of the family
